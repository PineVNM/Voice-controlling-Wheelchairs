# Voice-controlling-Wheelchairs

## Developed voice-controlled wheelchairs help increase the independence and flexibility of quadriplegic individuals, empowering them to live more self-reliant lives and participate more actively in their communities.
Achieved a speech recognition accuracy ranging from 73 to 92%, demonstrating effectiveness in understanding and processing spoken language.

Presented at the Sligo Engineering & Technology Expo 2023.

Tools Used:
- C++
- Arduino
- Voice Recognition
